Project 1: Interactive Audio & Visual with p5.js

Final: My House

my project is a house that plays the song/poem *my house* by nikki giovanni. the song is a favorite of mine and i like to listen to it to calm down while studying or after accidentally deleting 4 hours of progress in p5 because i forgot to log in... anyway, i was also inspired by My website is a shifting house next to a river of knowledge. What could yours be? One of her suggestions was a house, and it made me connect the idea of a website as a house to this song.

Final: What kind of girl are you?

my hypernarrative is a text-based click through game exploring themes of gender and fantasy. the player chooses between 4 life paths and explores how we think of different fictional archetypes. i was inspired by some of my favorite female led fantasy stories, like wonder woman, and wanted to imagine the intersection of how these characters are portrayed and conceptualized.

Project 3: Networked Care

Orcinus Orca

my site is an informational page about my favorite animal, the orca. the site covers history and facts about orcas, as well as current threats they faced and a brief of their cultural history. i find it important to highlight these creatures as they are often known for being featured in abusive shows but are otherwise underdiscussed in media.
